# Анимации Дракона

Схема: <тип> <элемент> <(<тип_анимации_1> [, тип_анимации_2])>

---
## Idle

* wiggle upper-arm 
* wiggle lower-arm 
* wiggle upper-head 
* wiggle lower-head 
* wiggle neck  
* wiggle body 
---

## Fire

* **faster** wiggle upper-arm 
* **faster** wiggle lower-arm 
* open upper-head 
* open lower-head 
* cran neck  (rotate_clockwise, move_right)
* cran body (rotate_clockwise, move_right)
* fire
---
## Sleep

* wiggle neck  
* wiggle body 

---
